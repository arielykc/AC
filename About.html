About

Welcome! My name is Ariel Choi and I am a sophomore at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am in the Hussman School of Journalism on the Advertising and Public Relations track and have a minor in Data Anlaysis. was born in Seoul, South Korea, but spent my life living in different locations including New York City, Paris, and Seoul. I went to Asia Pacific International School in Korea where I was part of the APIS Soccer Varsity Team, President of Abandoned Cats and Dogs, Fundraising Chair of National Honors Society, Founder of Business Marketing Workshop, Principle Flutist of Jazz Band, and many more.

At UNC, I am involved in the UNC Marketing Club and part of the UNC Morrison’s Community Government as the wellness committee and treasurer. Next year, I am planning to study abroad in London to learn more about Journalism. My interests in the journalism realm include advertising and marketing.

My hobbies include listening to music and playing the piano. My favorite TV shows are Gossip Girl and Suits, and my favorite movie is Devil Wears Prada. I currently live with my parents, my grandmother, and a Coton De Tulear
